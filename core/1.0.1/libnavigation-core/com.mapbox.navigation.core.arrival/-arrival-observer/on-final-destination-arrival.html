onFinalDestinationArrival

abstract fun onFinalDestinationArrival(routeProgress: RouteProgress): Unit

This will be called once the RouteProgress.currentState has reached RouteProgressState.ROUTE_COMPLETE. This means the device has reached the final destination on the route.
