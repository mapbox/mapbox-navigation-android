play

abstract fun play(speechAnnouncement: VoiceInstructions!): Unit

Will play the given string speechAnnouncement. If a voice speechAnnouncement is already playing or other speechAnnouncement are already queued, the given speechAnnouncement will be queued to play after.

Parameters

speechAnnouncement - VoiceInstructions!: with voice speechAnnouncement data.
